Mark Wise is a 44-year-old white male who is a detective in the Night City Police Department. With years of experience on the force, Mark has earned a reputation as a skilled investigator with a sharp mind and a steady hand. His dedication to justice and ability to solve even the most complex cases have earned him the respect and admiration of his colleagues and the community he serves. Mark's relentless pursuit of criminals and his commitment to uphold the law make him a formidable force in the fight against crime in Night City.
